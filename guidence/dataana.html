Data Analyst

Description

Data Analysts plan decision-making, improve efficiency, enhance customer experiences, and manage risks by interpreting complex data sets. Success in data analysis demands a blend of technical skills (data cleaning and statistical analysis) and soft skills (critical thinking and communication).

Career Path

Educational Qualifications Candidates who are aspiring must earn a bachelor's degree, ideally in statistics or information technology, with at least 60% overall or an equivalent grade point average (CGPA). Also, to become a data analyst, candidates must hold a degree in Economics, Statistics, Computer Science or Mathematics.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability
