Full Stack Developer

Description

The main task of a full-stack developer is to create a product from scratch and supervise the general flow of the project. To implement such projects, they require perfect knowledge in the field of the latest frontend, backend and cloud technologies.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications The eligibility for full stack development courses is 12th class in Science with English or an equivalent stream. Few full-stack development courses require students to have computer proficiency and basic knowledge of CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and Git
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

The average salary for Full Stack Developer is ₹8,20,000 per year in the India. The average additional cash compensation for a Full Stack Developer in the India is ₹1,20,000, with a range from ₹60,000 - ₹1,20,000.
