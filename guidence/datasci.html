Career Path

Educational Qualifications Candidates must complete class 12th in the science stream with Physics, Computer Science, and Mathematics as their compulsory subjects. Candidates need to clear the Engineering Entrance Exams in order to get into the top data science colleges in India, such as JEE Main, JEE Advanced and GATE.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Data Scientist salary in India ranges between ₹ 3.9 Lakhs to ₹ 28.0 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 14.4 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 40.8k latest salaries received from Data Scientists.
