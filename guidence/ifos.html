IFoS

Description

The responsibilities of the Indian Forest Services officers include managing the nation's national parks, tiger reserves, wildlife sanctuaries and other protected areas. The sustainable growth of the local fauna and flora is also one of the responsibilities of Indian Forest Service officials.

Basic Salary

The basic monthly IFoS Salary starts from INR 56,100 for the Assistant Conservator of Forests/ Assistant Deputy Conservator of Forests. The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Head of Forest Force) gets the highest IFoS salary of INR 2,25,000
