Goods Guard

Description

It is the goods guard who ensures the safe delivery of the cargo on the train. Some of the general duties of goods guard are: To ensure the proper working of all the brakes and emergency brakes at full capacity. To make sure the wagons are properly attached to each other.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications The aspirants can apply for the post of goods guard after the completion of graduation. The aspirants can pursue any course in bachelor's to apply for the post of the train guard. Post the completion of the entrance exam, aspirants will undergo training.
Age Limit: 18 to 33 years. Education Qualification: Graduation or equivalent.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability
