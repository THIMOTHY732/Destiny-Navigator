Inspector of Income Tax

Description

Your job would be the assessment of Income Tax which people or a company are liable to pay. Handling Refund Claims and TDS-related queries also fall under this job profile. In the Non-Assessment Section, you will be subjected to fieldwork. You will be a part of the rapid response team conducting raids.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications Anyone who wants to become ITO should be graduate and appear for the written examination. The maximum age limit for the written examination is 27 years upper age relaxation of 3 years will be given for OBC.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership, Financial Acumen
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

The 7th Pay Commission is responsible for setting the SSC CGL Income-Tax Inspector's 2023 salary. An SSC CGL Income Tax Inspector has a basic pay of about Rs 44,900. However, the in-hand pay for an income tax inspector might range from Rs 58,956 to 69,396 each month.
