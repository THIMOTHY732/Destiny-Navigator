Investment Banker

Description

Issues debt and sells equity to raise capital for clients. Conducts research on investment opportunities to define risk and return profiles. Assesses valuations for clients and upper management using various methods

Career Path

Educational Qualifications To become an investment banker in India, pursuing a bachelor's degree in finance, economics, or a related field is recommended. Additionally, certifications such as CFA or CPA can enhance your qualifications and provide an edge in the competitive job market.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership, Financial Acumen
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Average starting Salary for Investment Banker in India is around ₹2.0 Lakhs per year (₹16.7k per month). No prior experience is required to be a Investment Banker.Highest salary that a Investment Banker can earn is ₹55.0 Lakhs per year (₹4.6L per month).
