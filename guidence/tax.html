Tax Assistant

Description

The work of a Tax Assistants in the CBIC is totally clerical. Their work mainly includes dispatching and preparing some reports of the section he is posted in. Mostly they work under a Superintendent or Appraiser but in some places, he also works under Examiner, PO, and Excise Inspector

Basic Salary

The basic pay ranges from ₹5,38,800 to ₹17,08,800 per year, depending on the pay scale and level. Grade Pay: The grade pay for an Income Tax Inspector is ₹4,600 per year.
