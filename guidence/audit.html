Audit Officer

Description

AAOs play a crucial role by assisting Audit or Senior Audit Officers in conducting various departmental audits for Government organizations and Public Sector Companies. Responsibilities include making sectional decisions, preparing inspection reports, and reviewing department employees' bills for allowances.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications Any graduate who clears the SSC CGL exam for the AAO post can become an Assistant Audit Officer in the Indian Audit and Accounts Department under the Comptroller and Auditor General of India.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Audit Officer salary in India ranges between ₹ 1.5 Lakhs to ₹ 8.5 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 3.3 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 804 latest salaries received from Audit Officers. 0 - 9 years exp. 1 - 8 years exp.
