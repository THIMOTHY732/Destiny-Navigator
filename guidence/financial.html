Financial Analyst

Description

As a banking analyst, your job duties include analyzing data for banking portfolios, assessing areas like stocks and bonds, researching current credit trends, creating presentations to communicate your findings, and giving advice on the best investments for a particular client.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications Bachelor's degree in finance, accounting, economics, business administration, or a closely related discipline is needed for applicants. A competitive advantage may come from having a master's degree. The Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) qualification is well-respected in the financial sector.
Practical Experience Bank Operations Internship, Entry-Level Bank Roles, Advancement Opportunities
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership, Financial Acumen
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Average salary for a Financial Analyst in India is 5.9 Lakhs per year (₹49.3k per month). Salary estimates are based on 47.7k latest salaries received from various Financial Analysts across industries
