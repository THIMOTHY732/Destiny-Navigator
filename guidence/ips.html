IPS

Description

The IPS officers are considered as “Ex-officio Head” to maintain the district levels officers and be able to investigate all the incidents separately. This also includes investigation, crime prevention, counterintelligence, railway policing, drug trafficking, and corruption in the community's life.

Career Path

Process Step 1: Apply for Civil Services Examination
Step 2: Appear for Civil Services Examination
Step 3: Clear Preliminary Exam
Step 4: Pass the UPSC Mains Exam
Step 6: Clear Personality test
Step 7: Undergo the IPS Physical Training
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

The basic salary of an IPS officer starts at Rs. 56,100(TA, DA and HRA are extra) per month and can go on to reach Rs. 2,25,000 for a DGP. All civil services aspirants must also compare the IAS Salary with the IPS salary to analyse the difference between the perks and pay scale for the two posts.
