Career Path

Educational Qualifications To become an Independent Financial Advisor (IFA) in India, you must hold a Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or Business. You must also register with SEBI and clear their certification exams before operating.
Practical Experience Bank Operations Internship, Entry-Level Bank Roles, Advancement Opportunities
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership, Financial Acumen
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Financial Advisor salary in India with less than 1 year of experience to 9 years ranges from ₹ 1.2 Lakhs to ₹ 9.0 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 4.6 Lakhs based on 3.8k latest salaries.
