Inspector(Central Excise)

Description

Fieldwork includes collecting customs, central excise duties, and service tax on goods imported to or exported from the country through seaports and airports. They administer all the processes around it. They prevent smuggling and ensure CGST collection. An excise inspector has to conduct raids who fail to pay taxes.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications The eligibility criteria of SSC CGL Central Excise Officer is passed graduation in any field from a recognized university, there is no specific aggregate required in this case, aspirants in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply on a provisional basis.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Average starting Salary for Central Excise Inspector in India is around 8.0 Lakhs per year (66.7k per month). 4 years of minimum experience is required to be a Central Excise InspectoR.
