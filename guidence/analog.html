Analog Designer

Description

Analog design engineers design, develop, and maintain analog electronic circuitry systems. They will often work closely with R&D (research and development) to collaborate on innovative approaches to electronics system design.

Basic Salary

Analog Design Engineer salary in India ranges between ₹ 3.2 Lakhs to ₹ 35.0 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 18.7 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 546 latest salaries received from Analog Design Engineers.
