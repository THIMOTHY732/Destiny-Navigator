Bank Teller

Description

Serves customers by completing account transactions. Provides account services to customers by receiving deposits and loan payments, cashing checks, issuing savings withdrawals, and recording night and mail deposits.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications The educational qualifications necessary for a bank teller are: Graduation: A bachelor's degree in any discipline from a university recognised by the Government of India or any equivalent qualification.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Teller salary in India ranges between ₹ 1.6 Lakhs to ₹ 4.2 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 3.2 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 2.8k latest salaries received from Tellers.
