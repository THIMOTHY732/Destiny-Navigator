Junior Engineer

Description

Junior Engineers are responsible for the planning, execution, control and supervision of projects related to railway infrastructure. Junior Engineers are responsible for inspecting railway tracks, bridges, signals, and other installations of Railways to ensure their safe and efficient operation.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications Diploma/Degree in Engineering related to the post you are applying in the RRB JE exam. You must have a Bachelor's degree in Science with both the subjects - Physics and Chemistry along with a minimum of 55% marks aggregate.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

The RRB JE basic pay is Rs. 35,400/- per month.
