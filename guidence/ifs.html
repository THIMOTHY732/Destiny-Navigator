IFS

Description

IFS officers are responsible for establishing and maintaining diplomatic relations with foreign governments. They work to promote political, economic, cultural, and scientific cooperation between India and other countries.

Basic Salary

A newly appointed IFS Officer's Salary would be 15,000-39,000 INR. Senior Scales: IFS Salary for senior scales ranges from 15,600 to 67,000 INR. Super Time Scale: It takes 20 years for the IFS officer to reach this grade, and the salary would be 37,400-67,000 INR.
