Asset Manager

Description

Meets with clients to assess asset status, needs, risks, goals and progress. Prepares financial statements, business activity reports and forecasts.

Career Path

Educational Qualifications Asset managers require a Bachelor's degree in finance, business, or related fields. A Master's degree in business administration (MBA) may be preferred in some cases. Additionally, asset managers may need to possess a Certified Public Accountant title, which will require additional training and testing.
Key Qualities Decision Making, Communication, Leadership
Ongoing Development Perseverance, Continuous Learning, Adaptability

Basic Salary

Asset Manager salary in India ranges between ₹ 2.5 Lakhs to ₹ 12.0 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 6.0 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 185 latest salaries received from Asset Managers. 2 - 13 years exp.
