Loco Pilot

Description

The Loco Pilot is responsible for driving the train. At the same time, the ALP performs various supporting tasks, such as monitoring the train's speed and signals, operating the train's equipment and controls, and communicating with the train's crew and passengers.

Educational Qualifications The minimum age limit to appear for the Loco Pilot examination conducted by RRB ALP is 18 years, while the maximum age limit is 30 years.Candidates must have completed their 10th grade and possess an ITI certification. To secure a job in Indian Railways after 12th grade, you need to pass one of the numerous examinations conducted by the Railway Recruitment Boards such as the RRB NTPC, RRB Group D, or RRB ALP exam.

Basic Salary

The average salary for Assistant Loco Pilot is ₹1,54,000 per month in the India. The average additional cash compensation for a Assistant Loco Pilot in the India is ₹1,32,300, with a range from ₹24,600 - ₹2,40,000.
